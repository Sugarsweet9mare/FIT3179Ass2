Coffee Exports and Gross Opening Stocks

Gross Opening of stock prices exporting countries are plotted against Total Coffee Export of each country(In thousand 60-kg bags) in order to find if there is a big correlation between the two factors or does the quality and origin of the coffee beans matter more?
According to the scatterplot, there is only a vague correlation, suggesting that there are more factors than quantity that determines the price of coffee beans. The country that has the highest export and also gross opening stock is Brazil .While the lowest belongs to Nepal .
From the line chart of Total Exports each year independant of countries, we can see that the demand for coffee is gradually Increasing each passing year.
